Untested devices that may be compatible
=======================================

This remote was tested on a Hantech A018-12KR2/A portable air conditioner.
The devices below appear to be based on the same or closely related JHS
A018-12KR2 platform, so this IR file may also work for them.

Important: These devices are not yet confirmed. Please test before treating
them as supported.

Very high probability
---------------------

- Hantech A018-12KR2/A
  Exact label model of the tested unit.
  Source: https://www.kleinanzeigen.de/s-anzeige/mobile-klimaanlage-mit-fernbedienung-wie-neu-hantech-a018-12kr2-a/2779608701-176-4301

- Hantech A018-12KR2
  Same model family without suffix. SmartIR lists Hantech A018-12KR2 as an IR
  code set.
  Source: https://manuall.de/hantech-a018-12kr2-klimagerat/

- Hantech Tuya/WiFi JHS-A018-12KR2/A WIFI VDE PLUG
  Later WiFi/Tuya variant. It appears to use the same case and remote layout.
  Hornbach lists it as a 12,000 BTU / 3,500 W unit.
  Source: https://www.hornbach.at/p/mobiles-klimageraet-mit-abluftschlauch-hantech-12-000-tuya-weiss-schwarz-mit-fernbedienung-bis-raumgroesse-35-m-84m-lokales-klimageraet/10474165/

- MTF / Mountfield MTF AC 12000 / JHS-A018-12KR2/A
  Manual/EU declaration explicitly names model JHS-A018-12KR2/A, 12,000 BTU /
  3.53 kW, R290/230 g, 1357 W.
  Source: https://www.mountfield.cz/file/13436/navod-na-pouziti.pdf

- Lifetime Air JHS-A018-12KR2/A
  Pollin data sheet lists Lifetime Air with supplier model JHS-A018-12KR2/A,
  12,000 BTU, 1357 W, 42 x 36 x 72.2 cm.
  Source: https://www.pollin.de/productdownloads/D694010D.PDF

----------------

- ProKlima / Bauhaus JHS-A018-12KR2/C-W
  Same JHS-A018-12KR2 platform and 12,000 BTU class. Suffix differs from the
  tested /A model.
  Source: https://media.cdn.bauhaus/m/1268931/BH_DOC_1268931.pdf

- ProKlima A018-12KR2/C
  Home Assistant discussion describes this as a Bauhaus/ProKlima portable AC
  with Cool/Dry/Fan/Sleep, 3 fan speeds, vertical swing, and 16-31 C range.
  Source: https://community.home-assistant.io/t/proklima-a018-12kr2-c-portable-air-conditioner-fully-working-with-localtuya/544349

- Voltomat JHS A018 12KR2CW
  Likely Bauhaus rebrand of the /C-W platform, but source quality is weaker.
  Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=axNMnnwmtTs
